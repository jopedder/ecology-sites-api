🦇 Bat EPS Bat Mitigation Licences

The Natural England bat EPS mitigation licence register is not publicly available via any open data feed or spatial API.

To check bat licences for this location:

  • Search the local planning authority's public register for ecological reports on nearby planning applications
  • Contact Natural England — wildlife.licensing@naturalengland.org.uk
  • Search the planning portal for bat survey reports or EPS licence references

Bat mitigation licences are issued per development and not registered to a spatial location in any public database.
